Welcome to Mood Journal!

Thank you for installing the Mood Journal app. Follow the steps below to get started:

Step 1: Install Omi

Ensure you have the Omi AI device set up and connected to your account.

Step 2: Enable Permissions

During the app setup, grant the following permissions:

  • User Memories - For tracking your mood patterns over time.
  • User Name - For personalized notifications.

Step 3: Start Tracking

Once enabled:

  1. Speak naturally into the Omi device.
  2. Your conversations will be processed, and moods will be logged automatically.

Step 4: View Your Moods

Use the Mood Journal dashboard or app interface to view your emotional patterns over time.
